# Credibility Checklist

Proof assets that back your claims and increase Perceived Likelihood of Achievement.

**Rule**: Proof must be **contexted** - who it worked for + timeframe + conditions.

---

## Proof Classification

When building social proof, classify each piece:

| Type | Description | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Testimonial** | Customer statement | Addressing specific objection |
| **Case Study** | Before/after with metrics | Showing transformation |
| **Results Snippet** | Metric + timeframe + conditions | Quick credibility |

---

## Case Study Template (Contexted)

For each case study, capture ALL context:

**Who**: [Customer name/type]
**Starting Situation**: [Where they were before]
**What They Did**: [How they used the product]
**Results Achieved**: [Specific metrics]
**Time Frame**: [How long it took]
**Conditions**: [What made this possible/any caveats]
**Quote**: [In their words]
