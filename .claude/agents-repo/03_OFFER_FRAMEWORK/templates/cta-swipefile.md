## CTA Formula Templates

### Urgency + Benefit
> "[Action] now and [benefit] before [deadline]"

### Scarcity + Incentive
> "Only [X] left - [action] now and get [bonus]"

### Risk Reversal + Action
> "[Action] risk-free - [guarantee statement]"

### Transformation + Action
> "Ready to [transformation]? [Action] today."

### Social + Urgency
> "Join [X] others who already [action verb]. [CTA]."

## CTA Rules

1. **ONE action** - Don't give multiple options
2. **Clear verb** - "Buy" "Get" "Start" "Join" not "Click here"
3. **Immediate** - "Now" "Today" "Instantly"
4. **Benefit when possible** - "Get instant access" > "Submit"
