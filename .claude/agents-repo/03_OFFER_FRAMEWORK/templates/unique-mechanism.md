# Unique Mechanism Template

The unique feature that your product/service has that nobody else has on the market. Your key USP.

---

## What It Does

The unique mechanism answers: "Why does this work when other things haven't?"

It's not just a feature - it's the proprietary method/process/ingredient/system that makes results possible.

---

## Structure

**Name**: [Branded name for your mechanism]

**What It Is**: [1-2 sentences describing it]

**Why It Works**: [The logic/science/method behind it]

**Why Others Don't Have It**: [What makes this unique to you]

---

## Example

**Name**: Precision Targeting Blueprint

**What It Is**: A scientifically developed data-driven approach that identifies and engages the most lucrative market segments with tailored ad strategies.

**Why It Works**: Uses behavioral data signals + lookalike modeling to find buyers before competitors do.

**Why Others Don't Have It**: Proprietary algorithm developed from $X million in ad spend data.
