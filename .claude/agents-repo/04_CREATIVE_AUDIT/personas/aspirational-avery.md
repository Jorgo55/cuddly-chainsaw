- Dry technical tone
- Cheap-looking creative
- Generic stock vibes

## What She Needs to Convert

- Aesthetic coherence
- Narrative
- Identity upgrade
- "People like me" cues

---

## Proof That Works

- Social proof from "people like me"
- Visual transformation
- Brand cues
- Lifestyle imagery

---

## Language That Lands

- "Feel like"
- "Become"
- "The kind of person who"
- "Effortless"
- "Elevated"

---

## Evaluation Questions

When scoring for Avery, ask:

1. Does this feel aspirational or basic?
2. Is there an identity transformation?
3. Does the aesthetic match the promise?
4. Would she share this / be seen with this?
5. Does it feel premium or discount-bin?
