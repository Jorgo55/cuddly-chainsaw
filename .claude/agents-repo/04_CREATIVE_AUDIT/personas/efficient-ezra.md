# Efficient Ezra

**Archetype**: Low Friction / Overwhelmed

**Default Weight**: 25%
**Evaluation Order**: 1st (clarity/friction filter)

---

## Primary Drivers

- Simplicity
- Time saved
- Decision relief

---

## Instant Turn-offs

- Long explanations
- Too many options
- Complicated steps

---

## What He Needs to Convert

- "Do this, get that" clarity
- Minimal steps
- Clarity in 1 pass

---

## Proof That Works

- "Took me 2 minutes"
- Quick demos
- Checklists
- "Works out of the box"

---

## Language That Lands

- "Fast"
- "Plug-and-play"
- "In minutes"
- "No learning curve"

---

## Evaluation Questions

When scoring for Ezra, ask:

1. Can I understand this in one pass?
2. How many steps to get started?
3. Does it feel simple or overwhelming?
4. Is the path to value clear?
5. Would a busy person bail halfway through?
