## Primary Drivers

- Avoid regret

---

## Instant Turn-offs

- Aggressive hype
- "Miracle" tone
- No guarantee
- Unclear risk

---

## What She Needs to Convert

- Risk reversal
- Safety cues
- Specifics
- "This prevents X" framing

---

## Proof That Works

- Trust markers
- Warranty/guarantee clarity
- Calm testimonials
- Third-party validation

---

## Language That Lands

- "Peace of mind"
- "Avoid"
- "Protect"
- "Reliable"
- "Simple and safe"

---

## Evaluation Questions

When scoring for Paula, ask:

1. Does this feel safe or risky?
2. Is there a clear guarantee/reversal?
3. Are claims backed with calm credibility?
4. Would she feel stupid if this didn't work?
5. Does the tone feel trustworthy or salesy?
