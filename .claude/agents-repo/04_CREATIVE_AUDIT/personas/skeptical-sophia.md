# Skeptical Sophia

**Archetype**: Logic / Proof / Mechanism

**Default Weight**: 30% (highest)
**Evaluation Order**: 2nd (proof/mechanism filter)

> "I don't believe it" â†’ Second disqualification point for cold traffic

---

## Primary Drivers

- Truth
- Evidence
- Correctness
- Transparency

---

## Instant Turn-offs

- Vague claims
- "Everyone's doing it"
- Influencer fluff
- No mechanism explanation

---

## What She Needs to Convert

- How it works (mechanism)
- Constraints acknowledged
- Numbers with context
- Tradeoffs mentioned

---

## Proof That Works

- Data
- Demonstrations
- Before/after with context
- Methodology explained
- Comparisons

---

## Language That Lands

- "Here's why"
- "Tested"
- "Results vary"
- "Here's the process"
- "What most people miss"

---

## Evaluation Questions

When scoring for Sophia, ask:

1. Is the mechanism explained or is it "magic"?
2. Are claims specific or vague?
3. Is there data or just opinions?
4. Are limitations acknowledged?
5. Would a skeptic find holes in 5 seconds?
