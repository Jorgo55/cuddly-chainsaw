# Full Pipeline Workflow

Complete flow: Product → Avatar → Offer → Creative Audit

---

## Step 1: Load Product

1. Check `01_PRODUCT/` for existing product config
2. If none, fill `PRODUCT_TEMPLATE.md`
3. Confirm product details before proceeding

---

## Step 2: Apply Avatar

1. Check `02_AVATARS/standard/` for matching avatar
2. If none, fill `AVATAR_TEMPLATE.md`
3. Confirm avatar fit with product

---

## Step 3: Build Offer

Work through offer components in order:

1. `templates/value-equation.md` - Optimize all 4 variables
2. `templates/transformation-statement.md` - Core promise
3. `templates/unique-mechanism.md` - Why it works
4. `templates/offer-naming-magic.md` - M-A-G-I-C formula
5. `templates/pricing-strategy.md` - Strategic pricing
6. `templates/bonuses-playbook.md` - Value stack
7. `templates/guarantee-framework.md` - Risk reversal
8. `templates/urgency-scarcity.md` - Action triggers
9. `templates/credibility-checklist.md` - Proof assets
10. `templates/message-flow.md` - Persuasion sequence
11. `templates/cta-swipefile.md` - Call-to-action

Score against `rubrics/offer-score.md`

**Threshold**: Score ≥45/54 to proceed to creative (≥35 with minor fixes)

---

## Step 4: Create Creative

Use offer components to draft creative:
- Hook (from transformation + avatar pain)
- Body (mechanism + proof)
- CTA (urgency + guarantee)

---

## Step 5: Audit Creative

Run through `04_CREATIVE_AUDIT/`:
- 5 persona scorecard
- Conversion radar
- Trust score
- Verdict: PASS / REVISE / KILL

---

## Output

Complete package:
- Product config
- Avatar profile
- Offer components (filled)
- Offer score
- Creative draft
- Audit results
