# Offer-Only Workflow

Build offer components without creative audit.

---

## Prerequisites

- Product config loaded from `01_PRODUCT/`
- Avatar loaded from `02_AVATARS/`

---

## Process

### 1. Transformation Statement
Fill `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/templates/transformation-statement.md`

### 2. Unique Mechanism
Fill `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/templates/unique-mechanism.md`

### 3. Credibility Checklist
Fill `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/templates/credibility-checklist.md`

### 4. Guarantee
Fill `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/templates/guarantee-framework.md`

### 5. Urgency/Scarcity
Fill `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/templates/urgency-scarcity.md`

### 6. Value Equation
Fill `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/templates/value-equation.md`

### 7. Offer Naming
Fill `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/templates/offer-naming-magic.md`

### 8. Pricing Strategy
Fill `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/templates/pricing-strategy.md`

### 9. Bonuses
Fill `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/templates/bonuses-playbook.md`

### 10. Message Flow
Fill `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/templates/message-flow.md`

### 11. CTAs
Reference `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/templates/cta-swipefile.md`

### 12. Score
Score against `03_OFFER_FRAMEWORK/rubrics/offer-score.md`

---

## Output

- Completed offer components
- Offer strength score (/54)
- Identified gaps
- Recommendations for improvement

---

## Pass Threshold

| Score | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 45+ | Ready for creative |
| 35-44 | Minor fixes first |
| <35 | Rebuild core elements |
