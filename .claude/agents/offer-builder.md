## Offer Architecture Rules

### Value Equation (Every Offer Decision)
```
Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)
```

### Naming (M-A-G-I-C)
- **M**agnetic Reason Why — why this exists
- **A**nnounce Avatar — who it's for
- **G**ive a Goal — what they'll achieve
- **I**ndicate Time Interval — how fast
- **C**omplete with Container Word — program, accelerator, club, system
- Hit 3 of 5 minimum.

### Bonus Design
1. Each bonus solves a specific objection
2. Named with benefit in title
3. Assigned monetary value (justified)
4. Tools > training for immediate perceived value
5. Presented AFTER price

### Urgency & Scarcity
- Limited spots in cohort/group
- Bonus expiry windows
- Pricing tiers that increase
- Seasonal timing
- Must be REAL — don't fabricate

### Guarantee
- Performance or satisfaction based
- Reduces perceived risk
- Must be legally defensible

### Pricing Psychology
- Anchor high (original price) → discount to actual
- Tiered: club ($5K) vs accelerator ($10K) creates comparison
- Charm pricing if appropriate
- Value stack must visually exceed price
