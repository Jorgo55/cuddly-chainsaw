# Copy & Paste CTAs (Ben Heath community)

A small swipe-file of CTAs + what they’re good for. Replace placeholders like **[product]**, **[x]%**, etc.

---

## 1) Buy now and get free shipping
**Why it works:** Offering free shipping is an effective way to encourage viewers to make a purchase. It's a simple CTA that consistently works - people love the idea of getting something for free.

---

## 2) Limited Stock, buy now
**Why it works:** This tactic can create a sense of urgency, which is particularly effective for retargeting. It can motivate those who have been hesitating to make a purchase, increasing the likelihood of closing the sale.

---

## 3) Buy now and enjoy (product benefit)
**Why it works:** Emphasising the benefits is a powerful way to encourage viewers to make a purchase. People buy products to solve problems, so connect with them on a human level.

---

## 4) Get your hands on [product] before its gone
**Why it works:** Creates a sense of urgency, similar to a limited stock CTA, making it ideal for retargeting.

---

## 5) Order now and get a free gift
**Why it works:** People love freebies. If you can offer something extra with a purchase, and it’s within your budget, go for it.

---

## 6) Upgrade your [current product] with [your product]
**Why it works:** If your product is a well-known brand, this CTA can effectively showcase its benefits and persuade viewers to make the switch.

---

## 7) Invest in yourself with [product]
**Why it works:** Emphasise the value and impact of your product, highlighting how it can transform someone's life. This is my go-to CTA when working with self-care brands.

---

## 8) Shop now and get [x]% off
**Why it works:** Offering a discount is an effective way to encourage viewers to make a purchase, and this CTA highlights the opportunity to save more by acting now.

---

## 9) Get the product that everyone is talking about
**Why it works:** Used to create hype around your product and encourage viewers to make a purchase to be part of the buzz (this works super well on TikTok).

---

## 10) Get [product] today and never look back
**Why it works:** Emotion plays a crucial role in buying decisions. This CTA highlights the transformative power of your product, motivating viewers to take action.
