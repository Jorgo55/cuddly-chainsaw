- "over promised and ne'er delivered"

### Top Emotions Expressed
1. Exhaustion / burnout (most frequent)
2. Betrayal / broken trust
3. Loneliness / isolation
4. Anxiety / insomnia
5. Equity regret / loss of control
6. Frustration with ghosting / soft no's
7. Self-doubt / impostor syndrome
8. Financial desperation
9. Disillusionment with VC ecosystem
10. Family/relationship destruction

### Why They Sought Programs
- Couldn't raise on their own (rejection after rejection)
- Needed credibility/validation (stamp of approval)
- Wanted investor access/warm intros
- Looking for structure/accountability
- Needed co-founder matching
- Wanted to accelerate timeline
- Financial runway (grant money)
- Escape isolation of solo founding

### Expectation vs Reality Gap
- **Expected**: structured curriculum, deep mentorship, guaranteed investor access
- **Got**: 4 hours/week of events, generic advice, pay-to-pitch, mentor roulette
- **Expected**: support continues post-program
- **Got**: support evaporates after demo day
- **Expected**: fair equity terms transparent from start
- **Got**: hidden fees, changing terms, warrant traps
- **Expected**: founder-first culture
- **Got**: corporate sponsors as the real customer
