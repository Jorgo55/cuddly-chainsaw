## Component 3g: Guarantee

### Type: Implementation Guarantee (Performance-Based)

> **The Readiness Guarantee**: Complete the program, implement what we build together, and if your business doesn't have a documented operating system that passes investor due diligence standards — we continue working with you at no additional cost until it does.

### Why This Guarantee (Not Money-Back)

| Guarantee Type | Why Not |
|---------------|---------|
| Money-back | Too easy to abuse at high-ticket. Also signals "maybe this doesn't work" |
| Results guarantee ("you'll get funded") | Can't guarantee investor behavior. RISKY claim |
| Time-limited refund (30 days) | Not enough time to see results from a 90-day program |

### Why Implementation Guarantee Works

- Removes the fear: "What if I pay $5K and nothing changes?"
- Creates skin in the game on BOTH sides: we don't stop until it works, but they have to actually do the work
- Signals confidence: we're not hedging with a money-back; we're committing to the outcome
- Addresses the Expert Bottleneck specifically: "You tried a COO and it failed. We guarantee the SYSTEM works."

### Guarantee Terms (Clear)

1. You must complete the 90-day sprint milestones
2. You must implement the agreed-upon systems and processes
3. If after implementation your business still doesn't meet our investor-readiness scorecard — we continue at no charge
4. This is NOT "try it and get your money back" — it's "we don't quit until it works"
