## Executive Summary

**Verdict**: PASS

**Primary Bottleneck**: Social proof gap — no case studies or testimonials to back the "23 years / IPO founders" authority claim. Mechanism is strong but proof is thin. First-cohort problem.

**Trust Score**: 80%
**Weighted Radar Average**: 8.25/10

**Top Fixes (ranked by lift)**:
1. Add a live/interactive BIA demo or sample output to the mechanism section → makes the tool tangible, not theoretical (+0.5 believability)
2. Andy video intro or headshot with brief credential line in credibility bar → puts a face to the authority claim (+trust)
3. Prepare a "founding member" social proof frame for launch: "Be one of the first 15 founders diagnosed" → converts proof gap into exclusivity (+scarcity signal)

## Fix Plan

### Fix #1: Tangible BIA Output
**What to change**: Add a visual sample or partial screenshot of what a BIA report looks like — even a redacted example
**Why**: The mechanism is named but abstract. Showing the actual output makes it real. Sophia and Devin both need tangible proof the tool exists.
**Expected lift**: Believability +0.5-1.0 points

### Fix #2: Andy's Face + Credential Bar
**What to change**: Add Andy headshot or short video intro in the credibility section with verifiable credentials (LinkedIn profile link)
**Why**: "23 years working with founders" is an authority claim that needs a face behind it. Anonymous authority = weaker trust signal.
**Expected lift**: Trust Score +5-10% (Paula and Sophia benefit most)

### Fix #3: Founding Member Scarcity Frame
**What to change**: Add "First 15 founding members diagnosed this quarter" or similar above the CTA
**Why**: Converts the social proof gap into exclusivity. "We're new" becomes "you're early."
**Expected lift**: Avery identity boost + urgency for Devin
