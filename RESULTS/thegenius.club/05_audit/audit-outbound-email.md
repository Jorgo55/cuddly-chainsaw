## Fix Plan

### Fix #1: Trim Email 3
**What to change**: Cut body from ~120 words to ~80 words. Keep the pattern (pitch consultants → volume → neither works → BIA) but tighten each point to 1 sentence.
**Why**: Cold email readers scan. Email 3 requires too much reading investment for a cold list.
**Expected lift**: Click rate +10-15% on Email 3

### Fix #2: Soften P.S. Claim
**What to change**: "We've worked with 23+ founders who went from 'unfundable' to funded" → "We've worked with founders who went from 'unfundable' to funded. The starting point was always the same: find out what investors actually see."
**Why**: Drop the number unless it's verified. The qualitative claim is strong enough.
**Expected lift**: Trust +5% (prevents Sophia flag)

### Fix #3: Subject Line Testing Protocol
**What to change**: Test these 3 subject lines in batches of 1,500 each before full send:
- "Your 10th Investor Meeting."
- "Quick question about {company_name}'s fundraise"
- "The real reason investors say no"
**Why**: Subject line is 80% of cold email success. Test early, commit to the winner.
**Expected lift**: Open rate optimization — difference between 20% and 30% opens
